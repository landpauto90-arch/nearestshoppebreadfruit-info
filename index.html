FROM TREE TO TABLE

ABOUT BREADFRUIT

Breadfruit is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands. It can be cooked in many ways—boiled, baked, roasted, or fried—and has a mild flavor similar to bread, potato, or freshly baked bread.
